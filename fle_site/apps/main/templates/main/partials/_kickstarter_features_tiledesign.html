KA Lite

Kolibri will build upon our pre-existing KA Lite platform. This means that educators & learners can continue to utilize videos and interactive exercises from Khan Academy, along with KA Lite’s other features, including Coach Reports, data syncing, and self-paced learning.

Cross Platform Installation

To enable broad usage and maximize Kolibri’s reach, we’re building Kolibri to run on as many operating systems as possible, including Windows, Linux, and OSX.

Syncing and Sharing

Devices installed with Kolibri can find and directly connect with each other over a local wi-fi network, allowing for easy content sharing and data updates.

"Byte-Size" Videos

We're using an innovative video compressor which converts the video elements you see into scalable vector objects.

This is tech-talk for the idea that a 250MB video can now be compressed into just a single megabyte, while retaining most of the original quality. This reduces the time and cost needed to download a large set of videos, and allows a large amount of content to be stored on a small, low-cost device.


Select elements on the video by clicking and dragging your mouse around them. You can change their position and size!






Self-Paced Learning

Track educational growth for individuals and groups of learners accurately and easily, even in informal educational contexts.

Kolibri will be installed with evaluation tools such as "coach reports," customized quizzes and exercises, and a recommendation tool that displays useful and relevant content that is based on how a learner engages with their content.

★ Coach Reports: These are detailed progress reports that measure factors such as time taken to complete exercises, records on which questions have been answered correctly or incorrectly, and provide a picture on overall progress through a specific set of exercises and lessons.

★ Customized Quizzes and Exercises: Question sets and exercises can be created in Kolibri in multiple choice and free response format. This customized evaluation tool will help learners practice what they've learned and show what they know, while staying aligned with the goals and standards of their local curriculum.

★ Content Recommendation: Kolibri will recommend educational content to a learner based on the types of content and exercises they have previously engaged with.

Content Curation

Create your own "playlist" of educational content

How will this work?

Educators can curate content in 3 ways:

1

Use pre-existing channels, like Khan Academy.

2

Create a new, unique channel to match the curricular standards and objectives of a local area.

3

Take bits and pieces from any combination of channels (like making a playlist), to curate and align content and maximally meet the needs of the students in a particular area.
